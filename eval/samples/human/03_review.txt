Watched The Substance last night. Look. I get what it was going for. Body horror as commentary on the disposability of women in entertainment, fine, sure, on paper that's a film I would normally chew through happily. But god, it was so long. Like, by the second hour I was checking my phone. The Demi Moore performance is genuinely great — better than anything she's done in years probably — and the practical effects are disgusting in a really good way. But the script just hammers the same point over and over until you want to scream "I get it, beauty standards are bad, can we move on please." Worth watching for the last twenty minutes alone, which are completely unhinged and I mean that as a compliment. Three stars. Maybe three and a half if I'm being generous, which today I'm not.
