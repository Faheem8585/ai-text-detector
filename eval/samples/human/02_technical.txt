Spent the morning chasing a weird race condition in our Kafka consumer. Two threads were both committing offsets to the same partition under load, which led to messages being processed twice in roughly 1% of cases — small enough that it took us three weeks to even notice. Pushed a fix using a single-thread executor for the commit path. Tests are green but I'm not totally sold on the design. The right move is probably to shard consumers per partition so the contention can't happen in the first place, but that's a bigger refactor and we're already behind on the Q3 stuff.
